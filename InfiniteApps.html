How to unblock youtube


Step 1:
Create a new Google Slideshow
Step 2:
Click on the Insert tab in the slideshow
Step 3:
Select the "Video" tab in the insert section
Step 4:
Search for a Youtube video (Age restrictions still apply)
Step 5:
Click on the video

How to fullscreen the video
Step 1:
If you haven't already inserted a video, follow steps 1-4
Step 2:
Before you click on the video in the slideshow, it should start playing

Bring your own domain



BYOD, or bring your own domain is a way to have your own domain and have it point to Infinite Campus



How to set up

Step 1: Create a CNAME record for the subdomain, or domain that you want to be Infinite Campus
Step 2: Have the CNAME go to infinitecampus.xyz or www.infinitecampus.xyz
That's it!
