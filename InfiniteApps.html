    Frequently Asked Questions


    1. What Is The Difference Between The Website Chat And The Live Discord Chat?

    Answer: The Website Chat Does Not Pull From Discord, Only From A Server,
    However, The Live Discord Chat Pulls From The Discord And You Can Message To The Discord.

    2. How Can I Join The Discord If I Am On My School Computer?

    Answer: Here Is The Link To It, You Can Email It To Yourself And Open It On Another Device, Or Sign Into Discord In The Proxy, And Then Join It

    https://discord.gg/4d9hJSVXca

    3. Why Don't Some Of The Games Work In Method 2?

    Answer: The 2nd Games Method Uses Regular Iframe Embedding, And Does Not Go Through The Proxy.
    This Means That Those URLs Are Blocked For You.
    If You Would Still Like To Play Those Games, I Reccomend: Using The First Games Method, Or Finding A Different URL


    4. Why Doesn't The Google Form Work?

    Answer: It May Be Due To Your School Districts' Policy That Doesnt Allow Forms
    Or Emails Outside Of The District
    If This Is The Case, Feel Free To Reach Out Through One Of The Things Mentioned Earlier


    5. Why Do The About, Chat, Help/Support, And Download Games Buttons Do Nothing?

    Answer: It Is Likely That Javascript Is Blocked For You. If This Happens The Site
    Basiclly Becomes Useless, And As It Is Nearly Impossible To Code In The Games With
    Only HTML, I Recommend Not Going On This Site
